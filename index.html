About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Information of University of Michigan advised by Professor Paramveer Dhillon. Also, I am honored to work closely with Professor Qiaozhu Mei and Yixin Wang. Before that, I earned my B.S. degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University, transferred from the School of Informatics at Xiamen University where I majored in Computer Science and Technology. I've also been lucky to work with Professor David Jurgens at Blablablab and Professor Ruihong Huang in Texas A&M University.

I am broadly interested in natural language processing, machine learning, causal inference, human-AI collaboration, and computational social science.
